Rules of the game :

At every level the computer will flash one button and the user has to memorize the sequence starting from the level 1 and click the buttons in that order. For example :

  • If computer flashes the green button, click on the green button.
  • Now you are at level 2.
  • The computer now flashes the red button, but this time you have to click the buttons in sequence starting from level 1, i.e., click on the green button first and then the red.

Press any key to start the game.
